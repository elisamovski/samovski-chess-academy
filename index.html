Programs

Level-based training with a path forward.

Groups are small, structured, and focused on improvement. Ratings are guidelines, and placement can be adjusted based on a student's experience and readiness.

0-800 USCF

Beginner Group

For new and early-stage players building board vision, tactics, openings, checkmates, and good habits.

800-1100

Intermediate Group

For players ready to strengthen calculation, planning, pattern recognition, and tournament confidence.

1100-1400

Advanced Intermediate Group

For developing competitors working on deeper strategy, game review, sharper tactics, and consistency.

Private Lessons

Focused one-on-one coaching for targeted improvement.

Private lessons are available for students who need personalized support, game review, tournament preparation, or a flexible training option alongside group classes.

Private lessons are a good fit for students who need:

  • Extra confidence with fundamentals
  • Targeted help with tactics, openings, or endgames
  • Review of tournament or online games
  • A personalized improvement plan
  • Flexible scheduling outside group class times

About

Founded by Eli Samovski to help students improve with structure.

Samovski Chess Academy exists to help students build fundamentals, confidence, and long-term chess improvement. The academy emphasizes structure, consistency, and player development instead of one-off lessons without a clear path.

Students are guided through level-appropriate concepts, practice routines, review, and steady progression so parents can understand where their child is and what comes next.
